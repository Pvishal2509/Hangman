Word Guessing Game - Hangman
Description
This is a classic word guessing game built in Python. 
The game randomly selects words from different categories 
(Programming, Technology, and Games), and the player has to guess the word by suggesting letters, 
one at a time. For each incorrect guess,
The game continues until the player either correctly guesses the word or the hangman is fully drawn.

Features:
Word Categories: Choose from 3 categories: Programming, Technology, and Games.
Hints: Each word has a hint associated with it to help the player guess the word.
Score Tracking: The game keeps track of the score based on 
the number of attempts left and updates the high score after each round.
High Score: The game stores and displays the current high score.
How to Play
Start the Game:

Enter your name, city, and level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced).
Choose a Category:

The available categories are:
Programming: Words related to coding and software development.
Technology: Words related to gadgets and tech.
Games: Words related to board games and video games.
Guess the Word:

You will be given a hint and the word will be hidden with underscores.
Enter one letter at a time as your guess.
If the guess is correct, the letter will be revealed in the word.
If the guess is wrong, the number of attempts left decreases.
Win/Loss:

You win the round if you guess the entire word correctly.
If you run out of attempts, the game ends, and the word is revealed.
Next Round:

After a win or loss, you can choose to play another round or exit the game.
Requirements
Python 3.x installed on your system.
